Instructions:

  1. This is a game all about risk.
  2. Each die will have a value between 1 and 10.
  3. After each roll, you will be shown the sum of your two die.
  4. If you choose to roll again, if the sum of the two die is greater than your last roll, your money will double. HOWEVER, if the sum of the two dice is lower, you lose everything, and the game ends.
  5. You can cash out at any time. Once you cash out, the game is over.
  6. You start with $250
  7. To begin, simply press enter to roll the dice.
